Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about using our AI assistant, from setup to security. Still curious? Drop us a message and we’ll get right back to you.

What does the AI assistant do during meetings?

It automatically records, transcribes, and summarizes your meetings in real-time.

Do I need to take notes while using it?

No. The AI captures everything, so you can stay engaged without writing notes.

Is it compatible with Zoom, Google Meet, and Teams?

Yes, it works seamlessly with all major video conferencing platforms.

How accurate are the transcriptions?

Very accurate—our AI uses advanced speech recognition to capture every word clearly.

Can I access past meeting summaries?

Absolutely. All recordings, transcripts, and summaries are stored and searchable.
